Derealization

Derealization is a small proof of concept game made with a small group of 6 people. The game has the theme "Mental illness" & was put in the halls of SintLucas Eindhoven.

I was one of the 2 programmers & the level designer.

The design

For this game I was primarily focused on incorporating the mechanics in a way that feels natural. Our goals were to make the player feel a little uneasy, which is why we decided to make the game take place in a long and narrow tunnel, as it constrains movement. At the end of the hallway we put a seemingly endless lift, which creates more tention.

In terms of movement we had to make a tough decision between fluid movement which feels good to control or slower movement which would make the player feel even more uneasy.

My contributions

My contributions to this projects are the flying enemy that hones in, the Automatic lift & I assisted on the level design.
