Project_twister

Project Twister is a top down racing game made in Unity, using C#. Project twister, has a unique twist (so to speak) as the game doesn’t have standard steering controls. Instead of simply steering or drifting, the vehicle twists left or right and you have to let go of the button to go in the direction of the current orientation. This project ended up being a ton of fun to work on and I’m quite proud of it. I was the game designer and programmer.
