Project_twister

Project Twister is a top down racing game made in Unity, using C#. Project twister, has a unique twist (so to speak) as the game doesn’t have standard steering controls. Instead of simply steering or drifting, the vehicle twists left or right and you have to let go of the button to go in the direction of the current orientation. This project ended up being a ton of fun to work on and I’m quite proud of it. I was the game designer and programmer. Rocks by: Daniel Robnik Trees by: LoopTroop

The design

For this game I was mostly focussed on making sure the game felt unique and fun to play. The unique turning style and simple mechanics created a uniquely even playing field, as experienced players had to learn a new control style.

My contributions

For this project I did everything but the 3D modeling (which were obtained through the Unity marketplace).
