Project_Reversal

Project reversal is a game made in Unity using C#. The game was just a small school project I made in 8 weeks, but I was quite proud of the final result. The game is a 2D platformer with a unique gimmick. The unique gimmick is gravity reversal while also putting you at the bottom of the platform. While I created it with the intent of becoming a more action heavy platformer, it became more of a puzzle platformer. I was both the game designer and programmer.
